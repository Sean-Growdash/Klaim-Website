Appendix · Risk
Appendix · 4 of 4

What could go wrong  & how we mitigate.

A two-sided marketplace dies on cold-start, not on competition. Our mitigation is a supply-led launch — 30–50 premium venues locked before any consumer marketing.

Strengths

Moats from day one

  • Zero POS — kills the #1 B2B objection
  • Founder network · Growdash relationships unlock premium UK groups
  • App + dashboard already built — no tech execution risk in this round
Weaknesses

Where we're sensitive

  • Margin sensitivity to Open Banking API pricing (TrueLayer / GoCardless)
  • B2C CAC in London is expensive if Drop Buddies viral loop fails
Opportunities

Compounding upside

  • claim.co exit precedent — proven mechanic, proven outcome
  • SaaS Analytics shifts the multiple at Series A
  • EU expansion via single-API providers (Yapily)
Threats — & mitigations

Three scenarios we plan for

  • Cold-start: supply-led launch, 30+ venues before any user marketing
  • Restaurant churn: drop-boost low-volume partners to deliver guaranteed footfall
  • Bank-link friction: lean hard on FCA & bank-grade messaging

🔒 Privacy first

Your bank stays your bank.

We connect via FCA-regulated Open Banking — the exact same plumbing your bank's own app uses. Klaim never sees your card number, sort code, or login. We just see "did the transaction happen?" and send you cash if yes.

FCA regulated · Open Banking

The deal, in plain English.

  • We never see your card number, PIN, or login
  • Read-only — we cannot move money out of your account
  • Revoke access from your bank app any time
  • We don't sell your data, ever — that's not the business
  • Your eating habits aren't shared with anyone, full stop
Got questions?

Everything you need to know.

The questions everyone asks the first time they hear there's a thing that pays you back to do something you were going to do anyway.

What's the catch?

None for diners. Klaim is free, the cashback comes from the restaurant — they pay us a small fee for every diner we send through the door. Same logic as a Google ad, except instead of Google taking the budget, you do.

Which UK banks work?

All the big ones — Monzo, Starling, Revolut, Barclays, HSBC, NatWest, Lloyds, Halifax, Santander, Nationwide, First Direct, Chase UK, the lot. American Express support is coming Q3 2026.

How does the cashback actually arrive?

Via Faster Payments to your nominated current account. Usually within 30–60 seconds of you tapping at the till — often before pudding lands. Genuinely faster than splitting the bill.

What's a "drop" and when?

Every Monday & Friday at 9am, three personalised cashback offers land in your app. You pick one to redeem, trade it with a friend, or gift it to someone — before the next drop arrives. A deliberate ritual, not an infinite voucher feed of doom.

Can I really trade my Klaim with friends?

Yes — one of our most-loved features. If you receive a drop you don't want, offer a trade with any friend in London. They accept or decline; if accepted, your Klaims swap instantly. You can also gift one outright to a friend.

Where in London does it work?

Soho, Shoreditch, Borough, Marylebone, Hackney and Bermondsey are live now. Peckham & Notting Hill arrive in May. Heading for full Zone 1–3 coverage by year end.

Does it work outside London?

Not yet — going deep on London first. Manchester, Bristol & Edinburgh are on the 2027 roadmap. Add yourself and we'll text the second your city opens.

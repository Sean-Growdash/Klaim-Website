Operator FAQ

The actual demo questions.

So we figured we'd save you 12 minutes.

How do you know it was *your* cover?

Each Klaimer has linked their bank via Open Banking. When they pay at your venue, we see the transaction (amount, time, MID) and match it against the offer they redeemed. Cashback only fires on a positive match — so every attributed cover has a real, settled card receipt behind it.

Can we cap the campaign budget?

Yes. You set the campaign budget before launch. Drops go live on Mondays and Fridays; once your drop is live, the campaign stays active in that drop until the drop ends or the budget is fully utilised.

Do we need to change anything at the till?

No. Zero front-of-house changes, no QR codes, no waiter scripts, no integration with your EPOS or POS. You keep operating exactly as you do today.

How does the take rate actually work?

We take £1.50 per verified transaction, deducted from the cashback amount.

Can we kill it if it's not working?

Any time, no notice, no fee. Pause from the dashboard. Resume from the dashboard. We'd rather have happy operators than locked-in ones.

Will Klaim "train" diners to only come for the offer?

Data says no — repeat-visit rate is 35-45%, and the second visit is usually un-incentivised. The drop format means diners can't predict if you'll be in next week, so they don't wait for you. They just discover you, and most come back.

How do we get on a drop?

Book a 15-min demo. If it's a fit, you can be on the next Monday drop within 7 days. We onboard 8-14 London venues per month — currently a small waitlist for Soho & Shoreditch.
